The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

A powerful story about hope and friendship inside a prison. Why it’s great: Inspiring message and unforgettable ending.

The Godfather

A powerful story about family, loyalty, and the mafia world. Why it’s great: Iconic characters and strong storytelling.

Fight Club

A man struggles with identity and modern society. Why it’s great: Dark, intense, and unforgettable twist.
